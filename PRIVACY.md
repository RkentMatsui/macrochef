# MacroChef Privacy Policy

**Effective date: 13 July 2026**

MacroChef is a macro/calorie tracking and cooking app. It has **no accounts, no
ads, no analytics, and no developer-operated servers**. Your data stays on your
device unless you explicitly send it somewhere.

## Data the app stores

Everything you enter — food logs, recipes, weight entries, macro targets,
grocery lists, and settings — is stored in a local database **on your device
only**. The developer never receives, sees, or has access to any of it.

Backups (manual exports and the automatic safety backup) are written to local
storage on your device (e.g. your Downloads folder) and are entirely under your
control.

## Microphone and voice

Hands-free cooking uses **on-device** speech recognition and text-to-speech.
Audio is processed locally and is **never recorded, stored, or sent off the
device**. The microphone is only active while a voice cooking session or voice
logging is in use.

## Camera and photos

If you photograph a food or nutrition label to estimate its nutrition, that
photo is sent to the **AI provider you configured** (see below) for analysis.
Photos are not sent anywhere else and are not stored by the developer.

## Optional AI features

Recipe generation, voice-command understanding, and nutrition estimation can use
a large-language-model provider **that you choose and configure yourself**
(Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, Google Gemini, or Groq) with **your own API key**.
When you use these features, the relevant text (e.g. a recipe request or a food
description) is sent to that provider and is governed by *their* privacy policy.
Your API key is stored in Android's encrypted secure storage on your device and
is sent only to the provider it belongs to — never to the developer.

If you select the **local model** option instead, AI features run entirely
on-device and nothing is sent to any AI provider.

## Food database lookups

When a food isn't known locally, the app may look it up by name against public
food databases: **USDA FoodData Central** and **Open Food Facts**. Only the food
name/search text is sent. The optional downloadable nutrition pack moves these
lookups on-device.

## Downloads

Optional asset packs (nutrition pack, voice models, local AI models) are
downloaded from GitHub Releases or the model publisher when you request them.
These are plain file downloads; no personal data is sent.

## Data deletion

All app data lives on your device: clear the app's storage or uninstall the app
to delete it. Backup files you exported remain yours to keep or delete.

## Children

MacroChef is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect
personal information from anyone.
